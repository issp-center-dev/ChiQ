ChiQ
=======

.. only:: html

  ChiQ is a solver for calculating susceptibility :math:`\chi(q,\omega)` with the Bethe-Salpeter equation solver as a postscript of `DCore <https://issp-center-dev.github.io/DCore/>`_.
